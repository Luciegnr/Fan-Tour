History of England

England is the largest and most populous of the four nations that make up the United Kingdom. England is a territory conquered by the Anglo-Saxons in the 5th century, before being unified in the 10th century and forming the Kingdom of England, which becomes Great Britain by the Acts of Union of 1707 with the Kingdom of Scotland, then the United Kingdom by the Acts of Union of 1800 with the Kingdom of Ireland.


History of London

London is the capital and largest city of England and the United Kingdom with around 7 684 700 peoples. The city is located in the south east of England. London is considered to be one of the most important world cities in the world.


History of Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a series of films based on the book of author J. K. Rowling. The series is composed of eight fantastic films, it begins with the movie “Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone” and finished with “Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 2”.

Through these films, we follow the story of Harry a young orphan wizard who lost his parents when he was a baby. During the eight films we accompany him from his entry into the wizarding school and the discovery of his powers in the first episode until final fight against Voldemort the assassin of his parents in the last part.

In the first episode, Harry Potter enters at the wizarding school where he learns to master magic with his two new friends Ron and Hermione. In the second movie, a year has passed and Harry begins his second year in Hogwarts. He is then announced as the heir of Salazar Slytherin, the founder of the chamber of secrets. The third movie allows Harry to learn a little more about his past and his relationship with the prisoner, Sirius Black who escaped from Azkaban prison.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of fire is the episode where Harry will become one of the four candidates for the tournament of the three wizards. In his fifth years at Hogwarts, Harry will create a secret group, Dumbledore’s army, to lead a rebellion involving the Order of the Phenix. Voldemort is back and the fight is getting closer. In the “Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows”, Harry and the spirit of the black mage are more and more connected and the final battle is finally going to take place.
